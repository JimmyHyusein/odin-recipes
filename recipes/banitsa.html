Banitsa

Home

Description

Banitsa is a traditional Bulgarian pastry made with layers of filo dough filled with a mixture of eggs and white cheese. It's crispy, cheesy, and often enjoyed for breakfast or festive occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of filo dough (400–500 g)
  • 300 g Bulgarian white cheese (or feta)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup sunflower or vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Butter or oil for greasing

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 180°C(350°F)
    2. In a bowl, crumble the cheese and mix with eggs,yogurt, oil, and baking soda
    3. Lightly grease a baking dish
    4. Layer 2-3 sheets of filo dough, brushing each with oil
    5. Spread some of the cheese mixture over the dough
    6. Repeat layering and filling until all ingredients are used.Finish with a few plain sheets on top.
    7. Optional: Score into squares before baking.
    8. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown.
    9. Let cool slightly and server warm or at room temperature.
