Description

Tarator is a refreshing cold soup from Bulgaria, perfect for hot summer days. Made with yogurt, cucumber, garlic, dill, and walnuts, it's a light and healthy dish served chilled.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 cucumber (peeled and diced)
  • 1â€“2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup cold water (optional, to thin the soup)

    • Steps

    1. In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth.
    2. Add the diced cucumber, minced garlic, dill, and walnuts.
    3. Stir in olive oil and salt.
    4. Add cold water if the mixture is too thick.
    5. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
    6. Serve cold with a drizzle of olive oil and more dill or walnuts on top.
